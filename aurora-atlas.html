Storytelling · Climate

Aurora Atlas

Designing a calm, data-backed story that helps communities plan for resilient futures.

Role

Creative direction, product design, motion design

Timeline

16 weeks · Remote and on-site field visits

Partners

Civic researchers, community organizers, climatologists

Challenge

Climate action plans often feel abstract. We needed to translate complex models into a gentle, empowering story that invited residents to imagine, not fear, future change.

Approach

  • Co-created a shared narrative with community organizers to prioritize trust and cultural nuance.
  • Built interactive, scroll-based scenes that pair quiet motion with localized data to keep attention without overwhelm.
  • Prototyped responsive layouts and motion studies to ensure accessibility across devices and screen readers.

Outcome

The launch brought 2.3× more residents into workshop signups and was adopted as a template by two neighboring cities. The motion guidelines now inform how the team shares research across channels.
