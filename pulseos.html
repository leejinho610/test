Design systems · Health

PulseOS

Designing an empowering care platform that unifies clinical tools, coaching, and compassionate onboarding.

Role

Product design, design systems, prototyping

Timeline

6 months · Hybrid

Partners

Clinical advisors, patient advocates, engineering leads

Challenge

Disparate workflows kept clinicians and coaches from sharing a whole view of a patient. We needed a system that delivered clarity without sacrificing empathy.

Approach

  • Mapped care journeys and service blueprints to identify friction between intake, coaching, and clinical review.
  • Built a modular design system with accessibility baked into every component, enabling faster compliant releases.
  • Prototyped decision-support flows with realistic data to validate safety, speed, and ease with clinicians.

Outcome

Hand-off time dropped by 28% and new patient onboarding satisfaction rose to 4.7/5. The design system continues to support new features without fragmenting the experience.
